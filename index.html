Whether you're looking to camp under the stars, enjoy a peaceful hike, or simply unwind by the lake, MeadowLands Park provides the perfect setting for your outdoor adventure. Experience the joy of nature and create lasting memories with us.

HIKING LEADERS

Leaders for any hike trail wanted…

At MeadowLands Park, our Hiking Leaders are dedicated to providing an unforgettable hiking experience. Whether you're a seasoned hiker or a beginner, our knowledgeable guides will take you on scenic trails, sharing insights about the local flora and fauna along the way. Our leaders ensure safety while making the hike enjoyable and informative for all participants. Join us for a 2-hour session filled with breathtaking views and nature exploration for just $50 per person.

VOLLEYBALL INSTRUCTORS

Volleyball instructors for any and all levels of playing experience…

Get ready to spike, serve, and volley with our expert Volleyball Instructors at MeadowLands Park. Suitable for all skill levels, our instructors provide personalized coaching to enhance your game, whether you’re looking to perfect your technique or just have fun on the beach. Our sessions are designed to be engaging and supportive, ensuring everyone enjoys the sport. Book a 1.5-hour session with our Volleyball Instructors for only $40 per person.

ZIPLINE ADVENTURE GUIDES

Zipline adventure guides to keep you and your children safe during your experience…

Experience the thrill of soaring through the treetops with our Zipline Adventure Guides. At MeadowLands Park, our guides prioritize your safety and enjoyment, offering an exhilarating zipline adventure that’s perfect for families and thrill-seekers alike. With professional equipment and expert guidance, you'll glide over the forest, taking in stunning aerial views. Each 2-hour zipline adventure session is available for $60 per person, promising an adrenaline-pumping experience you won’t forget.
